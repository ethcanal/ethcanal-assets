## What ETH Canal Is

ETH Canal is a community-driven initiative and event series focused on the **technical, cultural, and institutional evolution of Ethereum**.  
Based in Panama, ETH Canal serves as a bridge between developers, researchers, startups, enterprises, and public institutions across Latin America and beyond.

ETH Canal is not just an event. It is an ecosystem built around:
- Technical rigor without unnecessary barriers
- Open collaboration across experience levels
- Responsible adoption of decentralized technologies
- Long-term community building beyond conferences

## Using the Assets Correctly

### Digital Use
For websites, social media, presentations, and digital documents:
- Use **PNG** or **SVG** files
- Maintain clear space around the logo
- Prefer dark or high-contrast backgrounds for readability

### Print Use
For posters, brochures, signage, and physical materials:
- Use **PDF** or **EPS** files
- Follow CMYK color specifications from the Brand Guide
- Do not resize or distort the logo proportions

### Working With Designers
When collaborating with external designers or agencies:
- Share **SVG or EPS** files
- Provide the Brand Guide PDF
- Enforce logo clear-space and color rules

## Logo Rules (Summary)

- The ETH Canal logo is an **imagotype**, composed of:
  - An isotype inspired by Ethereum geometry and Panama Canal waves
  - A logotype with “ETH CANAL” and the descriptor “PANAMA 2026”

- Do not:
  - Stretch, rotate, or redraw the logo
  - Change colors outside the approved palette
  - Remove or reposition logo elements
  - Place the logo on visually noisy backgrounds

- Always maintain the minimum clear space defined in the Brand Guide.

## Color Palette (Core)

The ETH Canal visual identity balances institutional credibility with technical energy.

Primary colors include:
- Panama Red
- Deep ETH Canal Blue
- Black and White for contrast and legibility

Accent colors are used sparingly to introduce motion and innovation, including electric blue and mauve tones.

Exact RGB, HEX, and CMYK values are defined in the Brand Guide.

---

## Typography

Approved typefaces:
- **Montserrat**. Primary. Headings and titles.
- **Poppins**. Secondary. Subheadings and emphasis.
- **Source Serif Pro**. Long-form editorial content.
- **Work Sans**. Digital interfaces and body text where serif fonts are not ideal.

Do not substitute fonts without prior approval.

## Patterns and Visual Language

ETH Canal patterns are derived directly from the logo system:
- **Canal Waves**. Fluidity, connection, movement.
- **Triangle Sequences**. Energy, direction, modularity.

Patterns should support content, not overpower it.
